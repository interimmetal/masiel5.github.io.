Consider publishing your relevant articles with us!

The USSJ is a student-run journal publishing a wide variety of writing related to Slavic studies. Undergraduate work is collected semiannually in the form of translations, essays, travel writings, and other relevant works. UNC undergraduates with an interest in any Slavic-related disciple are encouraged to submit their own work for inclusion in the publication.

You can do so on this page, please be sure to fill out all fields when submitting and only choose pdf or doc filetypes, as others cannot be displayed here. Your email address will be used to contact you when your article is ready for publishing online. If your topic isn't one listed, please include it in the additional information box. If you have any further questions, please contact us at ussjournal@unc.edu
