About

The USSJ is a student-run journal publishing a wide variety of writing related to Slavic studies. Undergraduate work is collected semiannually in the form of translations, essays, travel writings, and other relevant works. UNC undergraduates with an interest in any Slavic-related disciple are encouraged to submit their own work for inclusion in the publication.

Submissions are accepted only from current UNC undergrads and reviewed by our editors for publication. If your publication is not accepted, it can be resubmitted before the deadline. Submissions are compiled and released twice a year, in the Spring and Fall semesters. The USSJ is completely student-run, and interested students can contact exampleemail@unc.edu for more information or to participate.
